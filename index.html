About

My work focuses on creating predictive analytics and simulation engines to innovate Geointelligence products and services supporting decision-making processes for social benefit.

Currently, I work on combining machine learning, deep learning, bayesian statistics, and sensor fusion algorithms for multi-sensor satellite data to develop novel maritime intelligence products at MDA.

My background is in Computational Geomathematics. My expertise is in scientific computing, geophysics-driven (electromagnetic geophysics), and geospatial data-driven modeling and simulation.

Troughout my career, I’ve worked on a wide variety of projects at diverse R&D environments in Academia, National Labs, and Industry in Canada, Mexico, Spain, and USA.

I also enjoy creating and supporing innitiatives to connect Industry with Academia, to increase the representation of women and Latin American in Computational and Data Sciences, and to help catalyze the use and adoption of AI in Latin America.
